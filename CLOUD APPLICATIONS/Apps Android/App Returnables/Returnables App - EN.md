# Florisoft Returnables App Manual

This manual describes the use and configuration of the Florisoft Returnables App. The app supports registering, counting and verifying incoming and outgoing returnable packaging.

The app consists of multiple modules:

## Returnables Outbound

The Returnables Outbound app helps employees register, count and verify outgoing packaging such as pallets, crates and other returnables.

The module supports searching for orders, managing sessions, counting packaging in batches, and completing a session before the packaging is processed into the invoice.

Registrations can include quantities, comments and additional control data.

* [Returnables Outbound Manual](https://github.com/florisoft/User.Manuals/blob/main/CLOUD%20APPLICATIONS/Apps%20Android/App%20Returnables/Returnables%20Outbound/Manual%20Returnables%20Outbound%20-%20EN.md)

## Returnables Inbound

The Returnables Inbound app is used exclusively to register incoming returnable packaging. The employee selects a customer and order, counts received packaging in batches and verifies the totals before completing the session.

* [Returnables Inbound Manual](https://github.com/florisoft/User.Manuals/blob/main/CLOUD%20APPLICATIONS/Apps%20Android/App%20Returnables/Returnables%20Inbound/Manual%20Returnables%20Inbound%20-%20EN.md)

## Open sessions

An interrupted count remains available as a session. Florisoft provides filters, Backoffice controls and an FSQR code to find and resume that session in Inbound or Outbound.

* [Processing Open Returnables Sessions](https://github.com/florisoft/User.Manuals/blob/main/CLOUD%20APPLICATIONS/Apps%20Android/App%20Returnables/Processing%20open%20sessions%20-%20EN.md)
