# Florisoft TrolleyLoading App Manual

## TrolleyLoading

The TrolleyLoading App enables warehouse employees to perform the complete trolley-building process. The app supports finding and scanning orders, creating and managing trolleys, registering products and boxes, and completing a trolley.

Products can be placed on a trolley manually or by scanning a barcode. The app also supports corrections, reverse trolley loading, external supplier barcodes, additional packaging elements, address labels, and exception registration. The configured policies determine which functions are available.
